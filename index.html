Relax, Explore, Enjoy

Nestled in a beautiful valley in the Swiss Alps, our resort is the perfect escape from the mundane. Dining options include an Italian restaurant, and ski-in bars on mountainside terraces. Other amenities consist of an upscale spa, hot tubs, a gym and a climbing wall. Breakfast is available.

Resort Highlights

Amenities and Options for Everyone

Escape from everyday life and spend a few warm and relaxing days with us. Enjoy the refreshing mountain air. Discover the village and the surrounding mountains on foot or with our bikes. Visit our spa or ski runs during a winter visit, or take a guided hike during the warmer months. We offer something for every traveler.

A Second Great Highlight

We pride ourselves on providing year-round activities, from skiing in the winter to hiking in the summer. Our resort is here to make sure you have the perfect balance of relaxation and adventure.
